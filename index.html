WHO ARE WE?

Geistwear is a collegiate apparel company based in Los Angeles. Our company provides a collection of stylish and trendy contemporary men and women clothing by integrating today’s trends and pop culture.
Geist, pronounced guy • st, is a German word for spirit; being a fan is all about giving your team the spirit and energy to win. Driven by the vocation to make today’s trends and pop culture more accessible to the greater collegiate community, our brand is about keeping up with new styles and trends while providing all college students with an awesome selection of clothing to represent their universities.
